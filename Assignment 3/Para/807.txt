hurricane carmen  contents  meteorological historyedit preparationsedit hurricane warnings were issued along the coast while coast guard personnel went door-to-door on grand isle urging residents to leave the area from there through the coast of southwestern florida small craft were advised to remain near shore due to rough seas.16 offshore workers were removed from oil rigs.17 many mississippi citizens having experienced the destruction of hurricane camille just five years earlier quickly left their coastal homes.18 impactedit following the storm the name carmen was retired from the annually rotating list of hurricane names.25 however due to a change in the naming scheme in 1979 it was not replaced by any particular name.26 united statesedit the hurricane caused two fatalities in louisiana a utility repairman who was electrocuted while working on power lines damaged by strong winds39 and a motorist who was involved in a storm-related traffic accident.42 total monetary losses in the state was estimated $150 million.3 overall the hurricane spawned four confirmed tornadoes.43 one touched down near brandon mississippi destroying a barn and causing other damage.44 another struck kaplan louisiana injuring one person.45 the storm's effects in mississippi were described as minimal and were mainly confined to minor traffic accidents during bouts of heavy precipitation.46 light to moderate rainfall from the storm extended as far east as florida and georgia and as far west as oklahoma and texas.19 see alsoedit referencesedit external linksedit navigation menu personal tools namespaces variants views more search navigation interaction tools print/export in other projects languages
