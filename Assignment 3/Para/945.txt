expedition 45  expedition 45 was the 45th expedition to the international space station scott kelly and mikhail korniyenko transferred from expedition 44 as part of their year-long stay aboard the iss expedition 45 began with the arrival of soyuz tma-18m at the iss on september 11 2015 and concluded with the departure of soyuz tma-17m on december 11 2015 kelly korniyenko and sergey volkov then transferred to the crew of expedition 46.12  contents  crewedit see alsoedit referencesedit external linksedit  navigation menu personal tools namespaces variants views more search navigation interaction tools print/export in other projects languages
