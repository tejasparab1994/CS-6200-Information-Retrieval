hurricane grace jones album hurricane is the tenth studio album by singer grace jones released in 2008 and her first album of new material in nineteen years  contents  backgroundedit grace jones previous album bulletproof heart was released in 1989 and despite several comeback attempts throughout the 1990s her next full-length record would be released almost two decades later the singer had
