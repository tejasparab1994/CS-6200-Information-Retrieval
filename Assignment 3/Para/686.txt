cape lookout north carolina cape lookout is the southern point of the core banks one of the natural barrier islands on the atlantic coast of north carolina usa it delimits onslow bay to the west from raleigh bay to the east core banks and shackleford banks have been designated as parts of the cape lookout national seashore.1 cape lookout is part of carteret county and is located 11 miles southeast of beaufort nc cape lookout is one of north carolina's three major capes the other two are cape hatteras to the north and cape fear to the south the cape lookout lighthouse is located on the cape approximately three miles northeast of the point of the cape.2 referencesedit navigation menu personal tools namespaces variants views more search navigation interaction tools print/export languages
