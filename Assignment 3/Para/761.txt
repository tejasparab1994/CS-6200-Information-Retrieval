martinsburg west virginia martinsburg is a city in and the county seat of berkeley county west virginia united states5 in the tip of the state's eastern panhandle region its population was 17687 in the 2016 census making it the largest city in the eastern panhandle and the ninth-largest municipality in the state martinsburg is part of the hagerstown-martinsburg md-wv metropolitan statistical
