expedition 40  expedition 40 was the 40th expedition to the international space station a portion of the expedition 39 crew transferred to expedition 40 while the remainder of the crew launched on may 28 2014 from baikonur cosmodrome in kazakhstan upon achieving orbit approximately nine minutes after launch soyuz tma-13m delivering the remainder of the crew began a four-orbit rendezvous with the international space station soyuz tma-13m subsequently docked with the rassvet module of the iss at 1:44 utc on may 29 hatches were opened between the two spacecraft just over two hours later at 3:52 utc the expedition ended with the undocking of soyuz tma-12m on september 10 2014 the remainder of expedition 40's crew joined expedition 41.1 crewedit referencesedit external linksedit navigation menu personal tools namespaces variants views more search navigation interaction tools print/export in other projects languages
