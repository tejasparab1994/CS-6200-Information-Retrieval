freezing drizzle freezing drizzle is drizzle that freezes on contact with the ground or an object at or near the surface its metar code is fzdz.1  contents  formationedit effectsedit when freezing drizzle accumulates on land it creates an icy layer of glaze freezing drizzle alone does not generally result in significant ice accumulations due to its light low-intensity nature however even thin layers of slick ice deposited on roads as black ice can cause extremely hazardous conditions resulting in vehicle crashes see alsoedit referencesedit  navigation menu personal tools namespaces variants views more search navigation interaction tools print/export languages
