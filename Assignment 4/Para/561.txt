hurricane natalie grant album hurricane was the eighth studio album from contemporary christian music singer natalie grant it was the ninth album for her career and it was released on october 15 2013 by curb records.1 the album was produced by bernie herms.1 the album has achieved commercial charting successes as well as garnered critical acclaim from music critics  contents  singlesedit hurricane
