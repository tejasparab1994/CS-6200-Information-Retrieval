represents a return to the classic guitar-driven rock sound that the band were known for during the 1990s recording began in 2014 with newcomers johnny rabb and jesse triplett joining longtime members ed roland dean roland and will turpin in the studio to create the band's first album since 2009 it became the number-one alternative album for the week of october 24 2015 four singles have been
