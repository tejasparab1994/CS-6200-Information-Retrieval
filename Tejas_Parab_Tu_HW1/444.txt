href="http://ams.allenpress.com/perlserv/?request=get-archive&amp;issn=1520-0493">Online archive</a></li>\n</ul>\n</td>\n</tr>\n</table>\n<p>The <i><b>Monthly Weather Review</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Peer-reviewed" class="mw-redirect" title="Peer-reviewed">peer-reviewed</a> <a href="/wiki/Scientific_journal" title="Scientific journal">scientific journal</a> published by the <a href="/wiki/American_Meteorological_Society" title="American Meteorological Society">American Meteorological Society</a>. It covers research related to analysis and prediction of observed and modeled circulations of the atmosphere, including technique development, <a href="/wiki/Data_assimilation" title="Data assimilation">data assimilation</a>, <a href="/wiki/Model_validation" class="mw-redirect" title="Model validation">model validation</a>, and relevant case studies. This includes papers on numerical techniques and data assimilation techniques that apply to the atmosphere and/or ocean environment. The <a href="/wiki/Editor-in-chief" title="Editor-in-chief">editor-in-chief</a> is David M. Schultz (<a href="/wiki/University_of_Manchester" title="University of Manchester">University of Manchester</a>).</p>\n<p></p>\n<div id="toc" class="toc">\n<div class="toctitle">\n<h2>Contents</h2>\n</div>\n<ul>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-1"><a href="#History"><span class="tocnumber">1</span> <span class="toctext">History</span></a></li>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-2"><a href="#Abstracting_and_indexing"><span class="tocnumber">2</span> <span class="toctext">Abstracting and indexing</span></a></li>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-3"><a href="#References"><span class="tocnumber">3</span> <span class="toctext">References</span></a></li>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-4"><a href="#External_links"><span class="tocnumber">4</span> <span class="toctext">External links</span></a></li>\n</ul>\n</div>\n<p></p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Monthly_Weather_Review&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The journal was established in July 1872 by the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Army_Signal_Corps" class="mw-redirect" title="United States Army Signal Corps">United States Army Signal Corps</a>. It was issued by the Office of the Chief Signal Officer from 1872 until 1891. In 1891, the Signal Office\'s <a href="/wiki/Meteorology" title="Meteorology">meteorological</a> responsibilities were transferred to the Weather Bureau under the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Department_of_Agriculture" title="United States Department of Agriculture">United States Department of Agriculture</a>. The Weather Bureau published the journal until 1970 when the Bureau became part of the newly formed <a href="/wiki/National_Oceanic_and_Atmospheric_Administration" title="National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration">National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration</a>, which published it until the end of 1973. Since 1974, it has been published by the <a href="/wiki/American_Meteorological_Society" title="American Meteorological Society">American Meteorological Society</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-NOAA_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-NOAA-1">[1]</a></sup></p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Abstracting_and_indexing">Abstracting and indexing</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Monthly_Weather_Review&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Abstracting and indexing">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The journal is abstracted and indexed in <a href="/wiki/Current_Contents" title="Current Contents">Current Contents</a>/Physical, Chemical &amp;
