Australian region cyclone season</b> was a slightly above average Australian <a href="/wiki/Tropical_cyclone" title="Tropical cyclone">cyclone</a> <a href="/wiki/List_of_Australian_region_cyclone_seasons" class="mw-redirect" title="List of Australian region cyclone seasons">season</a>. The regional tropical cyclone operational plan also defines a <i>tropical cyclone year</i> separately from a <i>tropical cyclone season</i>, and the "tropical cyclone year" ran from 1 July 1993 to 30 June 1994.</p>\n<p>Tropical cyclones in this area were monitored by four <a href="/wiki/Tropical_Cyclone_Warning_Centre" class="mw-redirect" title="Tropical Cyclone Warning Centre">Tropical Cyclone
