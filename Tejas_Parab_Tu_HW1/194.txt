<b>BMKG</b>) is an <a href="/wiki/Indonesia" title="Indonesia">Indonesian</a> non-departmental government agency for <a href="/wiki/Meteorology" title="Meteorology">meteorology</a>, <a href="/wiki/Climatology" title="Climatology">climatology</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Geophysics" title="Geophysics">geophysics</a>.</p>\n<p></p>\n<div id="toc" class="toc">\n<div class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indonesian_Agency_for_Meteorology,_Climatology_and_Geophysics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>Its history began on 1841 with individual observation conducted by Dr. Onnen, the head of hospital in <a href="/wiki/Bogor" title="Bogor">Bogor</a>, and was established as a formal government institution on 1866 by the <a href="/wiki/Dutch_East_Indies" title="Dutch East Indies">Dutch East Indies</a> government by the name of <i>Magnetisch en Meteorologisch Observatorium</i>. The agency name changed several times and its current name was given on 6 September 2008.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Tropical_Cyclone_Warning_Centre">Tropical Cyclone Warning Centre</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indonesian_Agency_for_Meteorology,_Climatology_and_Geophysics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Tropical Cyclone Warning Centre">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>Since 1986 the BMKG, has run a Tropical Cyclone Warning Centre (TCWC), within their headquarters in Jakarta.<sup id="cite_ref-Jak_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Jak-2">[2]</a></sup> Over the next 12 seasons, the TCWC named and issued international warnings for the area from the Equator to 10\xc2\xb0S between 90\xc2\xb0E and 125\xc2\xb0E. In 1998, the <a href="/wiki/World_Meteorological_Organization" title="World Meteorological Organization">World Meteorological Organization</a>\xe2\x80\x99s RA V Tropical Cyclone Committee recommended that <a href="/wiki/Bureau_of_Meteorology" title="Bureau of Meteorology">TCWC Perth</a>, in Australia take over warning responsibility on an interim basis until the BMKG\'s staff had the training to run the TCWC.<sup id="cite_ref-Jak_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Jak-2">[2]</a></sup> TCWC Perth then took over the warning and naming responsibilities until the <a href="/wiki/2007-08_Australian_region_cyclone_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2007-08 Australian region cyclone season">2007-08 season</a> when they handed it back to TCWC Jakarta.<sup id="cite_ref-Jak_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Jak-2">[2]</a></sup> The first depression to be named by TCWC Jakarta came later that year when Cyclone Durga became a Tropical Cyclone within their area of responsibility.<sup id="cite_ref-Jak_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Jak-2">[2]</a></sup> During the next two seasons TCWC Jakarta, monitored several tropical cyclones in the <a href="/wiki/Typhoon" title="Typhoon">North Western Pacific Ocean</a> and the Australian region. At the start of the 2010-11 season, TCWC Jakarta\'s area of responsibility was then extended out to include the region from the Equator to 10\xc2\xb0S between 125\xc2\xb0E and 141\xc2\xb0E.</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="References">References</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indonesian_Agency_for_Meteorology,_Climatology_and_Geophysics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: References">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<div class="reflist" style="list-style-type: decimal;">\n<div class="mw-references-wrap">\n<ol class="references">\n<li id="cite_note-1"><span class="mw-cite-backlink"><b><a href="#cite_ref-1">^</a></b></span> <span class="reference-text"><span class="languageicon">(in Indonesian)</span> <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.bmg.go.id/dataDetail.bmg?Jenis=Teks&amp;IDS=5408481373414691018&amp;IDD=2245610808159598900">BMG becomes BMKG</a></span></li>\n<li id="cite_note-Jak-2"><span class="mw-cite-backlink">^ <a href="#cite_ref-Jak_2-0"><sup><i><b>a</b></i></sup></a> <a href="#cite_ref-Jak_2-1"><sup><i><b>b</b></i></sup></a> <a href="#cite_ref-Jak_2-2"><sup><i><b>c</b></i></sup></a> <a href="#cite_ref-Jak_2-3"><sup><i><b>d</b></i></sup></a></span> <span class="reference-text"><cite class="citation web"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.wmo.int/pages/prog/www/tcp/documents/RAVTCC-12FinalReport_AppIV_CountryReports.pdf">"The Operation of Jakarta Tropical Cyclone Warning Centre (TCWC Jakarta)"</a> <span style="font-size:85%;">(PDF)</span>. <i>Indonesian Meteorological, Climatological and Geophysical Agency</i>.
