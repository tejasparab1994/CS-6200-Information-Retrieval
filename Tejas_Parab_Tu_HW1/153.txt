For example, the primary circulation of Earth\'s atmosphere is <a href="/wiki/Zonal_and_meridional" title="Zonal and meridional">zonal</a>. If however a parcel of air, that moves in a purely zonal direction, is accelerated or decelerated zonally, the <a href="/wiki/Coriolis_force" title="Coriolis force">Coriolis force</a> will add a <a href="/wiki/Meridional" class="mw-redirect" title="Meridional">meridional</a> component to its velocity.
