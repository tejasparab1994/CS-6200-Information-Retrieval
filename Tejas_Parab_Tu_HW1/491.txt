of the Pacific High west of Peru and Chile</div>\n</div>\n</div>\n<p>The <b>South Pacific High</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Subtropics" title="Subtropics">subtropical</a> <a href="/wiki/Anticyclone" title="Anticyclone">anticyclone</a> located in the southeast <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Ocean" title="Pacific Ocean">Pacific Ocean</a>. The area of high <a href="/wiki/Atmospheric_pressure" title="Atmospheric pressure">atmospheric pressure</a> and the presence of the <a href="/wiki/Humboldt_Current" title="Humboldt Current">Humboldt Current</a> in the underlying ocean make the west coast of <a href="/wiki/Peru" title="Peru">Peru</a> and northern <a href="/wiki/Chile" title="Chile">Chile</a> extremely arid.
